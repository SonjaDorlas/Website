IDENTITY

IADT GRAD SHOW

2016: College Project YR3

This project required me to design a mock visual identity for the IADT Gradshow 2016.
I decided to expand on the theme Cutting Edge, highlighting the college's unique intergration of technology into its courses. The concept for the campaign was to create striking images to reinforce this theme. The central image of the hand has six fingers, each representing a course with
its own identifying colour.
