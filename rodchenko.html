BOOK & POSTER DESIGN

RODCHENKO IS A REDHEAD.

2014: College Project YR2

The purpose of the project was to design
visual material to advertise and accompany
an upcoming exhibition on the works of the Russian Constructivist – Rodchenko. The book and posters act as a visual parody of constructivist propaganda. The seriousness
of the movement has been removed through the use of bright colours and playful imagemaking. Within the pages of the
pull-out book are a variety of personalities
from the constructivist movement.
