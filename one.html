UI/UX DESIGN

ONEVIEW HEALTHCARE

1st place ‘The ONE Design Awards 2016’

This interface is designed to enhance a child’s stay at a hospital as well as benefit the staff. The product revolves around a character that the child creates and develops throughout their stay. The game theme is dispersed throughout, giving hospital staff the option to set tasks and reward the child with a game item for their character upon completion. As the child gets rewarded for the completion of a task they will be more motivated to complete another. Children’s characters can compete in the game to gain a position on the hospital’s leaderboard which anonymously shows the game’s highscoring characters.
